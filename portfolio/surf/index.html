About Us

About Our Surfing Academy

We're passionate about sharing the thrill of surfing with everyone who visits or lives in Sri Lanka. Since 2017, we've been teaching thousands of students from around the world to ride the perfect waves at Sri Lanka's best surf destinations.

Unlock Your Surfing Potential with Expert Instruction

We are a team of dedicated, internationally-certified surfing instructors committed to delivering safe, professional, and unforgettable surfing experiences at Sri Lanka's most beautiful beaches.

From the golden shores of Arugambe to the consistent swells of Mirissa and the hidden gems of Unawatuna, we know every wave, current, and beach break like the back of our hands. We don't just teach you to catch waves—we teach you to read the ocean, understand wave mechanics, and become a confident surfer who can enjoy Sri Lanka's coastline for life.

Our students come from all corners of the world: backpackers seeking adventure, families looking for new experiences, and serious surfers wanting to refine their skills. What unites them all is the passion and encouragement they receive from our instructors during every single lesson.

Programs

Our Surfing Programs & Packages

We offer comprehensive surfing lessons tailored to every level. Choose the package that fits your schedule and goals, and let's get you on the board.

Beginner Surfing Lessons

Perfect for first-timers. Learn beach safety, paddling techniques, pop-up mechanics, and basic wave reading. Our instructors break down every movement to ensure you build a strong foundation. Includes board and personalized coaching.

Book Now

Intermediate Programs

Ready to progress? Improve your turning techniques, learn carving and cutbacks, understand wave selection, and build fitness. Ideal for surfers who've completed beginner lessons or have prior surfing experience.

Learn More

Advanced Technique Training

Refine advanced maneuvers: aerials, barrel rolling, and progressive turns. For experienced surfers looking to push their abilities. Small groups with personalized feedback from senior instructors.

Why Partner With Us

All our instructors are ISA (International Surfing Association) certified with years of professional teaching experience. You're learning from the best, not from casual surfers.
